004 / FAQ

Questions

How accurate will my figurine look? +
Your figurine clearly captures your pose, body language, clothing details, and overall likeness. It's a detailed personal sculpture — recognizable and personal, not a hyper-realistic replica. We always show real before/after examples so you know exactly what to expect before ordering.
How long does production take? +
Estimated 7–14 days from when we receive your photo. We'll keep you updated via WhatsApp throughout the process.
How does the RGB lighting work? +
Glow tier figurines include an LED base with an ESP32-C3 controller running open-source WLED firmware. Download the free WLED app (iOS/Android), connect to the figurine's WiFi hotspot, and control millions of colors and effects from your phone. Powered via USB — plug into any charger, power bank, or TV port.
Can I have more than one person in the figurine? +
Yes! Multi-person figurines are one of our most popular options — couples, parent-child, and group of friends all work great. Add +RM49 per extra person. We can also add a pet companion for +RM39.
What photo should I send? +
Best results come from well-lit photos with the full body visible (head to toe), in the pose you want captured. Avoid heavily backlit or blurry photos. We'll let you know if we need a better shot before starting.
What is your refund policy? +
Because each figurine is made-to-order from your photo, we do not offer refunds once production has started. If there's a production defect or shipping damage, we will remake or compensate accordingly. Contact us via WhatsApp immediately if there's an issue.
